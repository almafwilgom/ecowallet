Our Story

Built to reward sustainable action

EcoWallet connects households, agents, and communities to make recycling a habit that pays off. We believe everyone should benefit from protecting the planet, not just feel good about it.

Mission

Make recycling accessible, transparent, and rewarding through a trusted ecosystem of users, agents, and local partners.

Vision

A circular economy where waste is a resource and every person can earn by doing the right thing for the environment.
